Players On Air Presents:

Sister Act

Sister Act is the feel-good musical comedy smash based on the hit 1992 film! With original music by Alan Menken, this uplifting musical follows Deloris Van Cartier, a disco diva forced into hiding in a convent. Her vibrant personality and voice uplift the community and the choir alike. Get ready to rejoice in this energetic and heartfelt show!

Review Summary

Players On Air delivers a high-energy, heartfelt rendition of Sister Act. The cast brought laughter and emotion, with lead performances that captivated the audience.

  • Standout vocals and harmonies in the choir scenes.
  • Set transitions were smooth and effective, though minimalist.
  • Minor lighting issues didn’t take away from the overall charm.

This production proves that community theatre can achieve professional-level performances. Sister Act's message of transformation and unity resonated well with a diverse crowd. Highly recommended for families and musical enthusiasts alike!
